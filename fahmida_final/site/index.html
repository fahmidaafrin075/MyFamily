Our Immigration Story

In 2014 we move to USA from Bangladesh for the first time. It was 10th August, we landed at JFK airport and it was me and my brother's first airplane journey. We were so excited and felt very adventurous!! We start leaving in a two bedroom apartment in Brooklyn. It was a big dream to us to live in this city of New York!!
